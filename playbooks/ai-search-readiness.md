# AI Search Readiness Playbook

Use when the user asks how to prepare pages for AI Overviews, AI Mode or AI-driven Search experiences.

## Required Spec

- target pages or topic cluster;
- business goal;
- content type;
- snippet/indexing controls;
- available Search data.

## Process

1. Confirm pages are crawlable, indexable and snippet-eligible.
2. Check whether key answers are visible in text.
3. Map subtopics that query fan-out could explore.
4. Review internal links between supporting pages.
5. Review original evidence, examples, data and media.
6. Review structured data.
7. Review content controls and tradeoffs.
8. Produce action plan.

## Output

```text
AI Search readiness:
Current blockers:
Content improvements:
Technical improvements:
Internal linking opportunities:
Snippet/control tradeoffs:
Measurement plan:
```

## Guardrail

Do not recommend fake AI-specific requirements. Regular Google Search SEO fundamentals still apply.
